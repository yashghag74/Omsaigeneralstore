Our Story

Om Sai General Store has been serving the community of Bhopar Gaon, Dombivali for years with dedication and commitment. We started with a simple vision: to provide quality products at affordable prices to our local community.

Over the years, we have grown to become the most trusted retailer in the area, offering a wide range of products from daily essentials to specialty items. Our success is built on the trust and loyalty of our valued customers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the preferred shopping destination for the residents of Bhopar Gaon and surrounding areas by providing exceptional value, quality products, and outstanding customer service. We strive to make shopping convenient, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.
